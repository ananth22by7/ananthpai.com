Scoring Music

How well have music genres performed in the last 25 years?

This information visualization analyzes the influence of music genres by observing the critical performance of albums in the last twenty five years. For the years 1991 to 2015, the ten most critically acclaimed albums for every year have been chosen and rated on a scale.

For more information on Scoring Music, check out the project on Behance
